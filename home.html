What does empowering the nation do?



Empowering the nation is an educational organization established in 2018 that focuses on empowering domestic workers and gardeners through specialized training.Based in Johannesburg,the company offers two types of programs:six-month learnerships,conducted over 12 weeks, and six-weeks short skills training programmes. These courses are designed to provide participants with enchanced skills,making them more competitive in the job market.
